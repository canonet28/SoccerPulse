Common Issues

  1. Live matches not loading

    Check internet connectivity and reopen the app. Confirm you are on the latest app version.

  2. Timeline or replay not updating

    Force close and reopen the app. On TestFlight, ensure the latest build is installed.

  3. Export/share issue

    Confirm required permissions and retry after reopening the current screen.

  4. Archive behavior questions

    Archived pulse availability depends on backend persistence and retention settings.
