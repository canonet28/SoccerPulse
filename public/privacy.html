SoccerPulse Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 19, 2026

SoccerPulse ("we", "our", "us") provides a live football fan reaction app. This policy explains what information we process and how we use it.

1. Information We Process

  • Match interaction data, including emoji taps, match IDs, timeline slice/minute context, and aggregate pulse data.
  • Technical data used for reliability, debugging, and security, such as request metadata and error logs.
  • No account creation is required to use core app features.

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate live timelines and archived/replay features.
  • Improve app performance, stability, and abuse prevention.
  • Maintain and troubleshoot backend services.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may use infrastructure providers (hosting, database, distribution) solely to run the service.

4. Data Retention

Live operational data may be short-lived. Archived and aggregate match data may be retained to support replay/history features, then removed when no longer needed.

5. Children's Privacy

SoccerPulse is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure.

7. Your Choices

You can stop data processing by uninstalling the app. For privacy requests, contact us at admin@rusty-league.net.

8. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Updates will be posted with a revised effective date.
